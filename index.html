One Small Voice:
Virtual Cabaret and Community Night
Friday, April 23, 2021
7:00 pm to 8:30pm.
RSVP
Come join us for a virtual cabaret! Megan will be singing and playing some songs she's been working on with her voice teacher--the lovely Kate Merryman at Musicologie.
Since Megan has to sing by herself--for now--we would like to invite you to participate in the evening in a few different ways. You can . . .

Make some art:

  • knit, paint, draw, crochet, or otherwise create arts and crafts while you are listening to the cabaret.
  • We'll have a break during the show for people to share their artistic creations with the group if they like.

Chat:

  • participate by answering questions we'll be asking throughout the evening in the chat.

Serve a community:

  • donate to the Homeless Families Foundation
  • We will set a goal for donations and see if we can hit it together. We'll check in on the donations throughout the evening.

Sing along:

  • join us in singing as part of a song circle. Don't worry, you will stay muted.
Note: most of the songs Megan will be singing are clean. There are a couple songs that use explicit language, and one song that contains some adult references. Those two songs are What Baking Can Do from Waitress, and I'm Breaking Down from Falsettos.

We hope to see you there!
