One Small Voice:
Virtual Cabaret and Community Night
Friday, April 23, 2021
7:00pm to 8:30pm.
RSVP
Come join us for a virtual cabaret! Megan will be singing and playing some songs she's been working on with her voice teacher, the lovely Kate Merryman at Musicologie.
Since Megan has to sing by herself (this time) we would like to invite you to participate in the evening in a few different ways.

Make some art:

  • We invite you to knit, paint, draw, crochet, or otherwise create while you are listening to the cabaret.
  • We'll have a break during the show for you to share your creations with the group, if you feel like sharing that evening.

Chat:

  • We invite you to participate by answering questions we'll be asking throughout the evening in the chat.

Serve a community:

  • If you can, please donate to the Homeless Families Foundation with us. Team Overly will start the evening off with a donation.
  • We will set a goal for donations and see if we can hit it together. We'll check in on how close we are to reaching our goal throughout the evening.

Sing along:

  • We invite you to join us in singing as part of a song circle. Don't worry, you will stay muted.
Note: Most of the songs Megan will be singing are clean. There are a couple songs that use explicit language, and one song that contains some adult references. Those two songs are What Baking Can Do from Waitress, and I'm Breaking Down from Falsettos.

We hope to see you there!
